Pilau recipes


Pilau is one of the most common foods along the coastal region in kenya. Its one of the favourite foods of most kenyans especially the Swahili Lets take a deep dive on how to prepare this meal.


Ingredients

  • 1kg of basmati rice
  • 2 litres of water
  • Cooking oil
  • 1/2 kg beef
  • Pilau masala
  • 4 diced onions
  • 1 diced tomato
  • salt to taste

Directions

  1. To a preheated pan add a good amount of cooking oil and add the onions to cook for 5 minutes
  2. Once totally brown, add the diced tomato and cook for 2 minutes
  3. Add the pilau masala and salt to taste
  4. Add the meat and let it cook for 20 minutes till soft and tender
  5. Once the meat is cooked, add the rice and stir till all the rice gets the brown colour.
  6. Add the 2 litres of water and cook under low heat for 30 minutes.
  7. Serve with your favourite drink

Enjoy your meal
