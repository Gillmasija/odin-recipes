Supaghetti Recipe

Supaghetti is a wholesome meal made from wheat. This is one of the easiest and fastest meal to prepare. If you are a person with a busy schedule this is for you. prepare a delicious meal in 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of suphaghetti
  • 1 diced onion
  • 2 diced tomatos
  • 10g tomato paste
  • 500g minced meat

Directions

  1. In a pan with boiling water, add the pack of suphaghetti undiced
  2. Let it cook for 5 minutes and using a spoon submerge the uncooked parts of the suphaghetti into the boiling water and let it cook properly.
  3. Using a sieve remove the suphaghetti from the hot water and rinse with cold water to prevent overcooking.
  4. In a different pan add the onions to preheated oil and allow to cook till golden brown.
  5. Add the tomatoes and let it cook into a paste
  6. Add salt to taste and the minced meat, let it cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add enough water for soup then add the suphaghetti.
  8. Mix the suphaghetti and the meat source till properly mixed.
  9. Serve while still hot

Enjoy your meal
