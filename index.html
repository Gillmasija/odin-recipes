Meat Recipe


This is the famous meat recipe cooked in majority of kenyan homes. Meat is a great source of protein especialy for children. Proteins are an essential need in the human body. This is essential for growth ad development in the body. Here is an easy recipe to cook this meal.


Ingredients

  • 1 kg meat cubes
  • 1/2 kg potatoes
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 2 onions
  • 1 garlic
  • 10g tomato paste
  • 1 cup cooking oil
  • 2 tablespoons of salt
  • 1 cup of water
  • Desired spice(masala,soy source,curry powder,paprika)

Directions

  1. Preheat a pan over fire and add the cup of oil.
  2. Add the meat cubes into the hot oil and allow to cook over medium heat until they turn brown.
  3. Take out the now ready meat cubes and leave them to cool
  4. In the same oil add the two diced onions and let them cook till golden brown.
  5. Add one clover of garlic and cook for alf a minute.
  6. Add the tomatoes and cook till it forms a paste then add all the tomato paste and then d the salt
  7. Add the diced potatoes to the paste and let it cook for 5 minutes then add the meat.
  8. Allow this mixture to cook under low heat
  9. Serve while hot with any side dish of your choice.
